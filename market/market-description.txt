Have your Mintcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Mintcoin Wallet is the first mobile Mintcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Mintcoin amount in MINT and mMINT.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Mintcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Mintcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Mintcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Mintcoin balance.

You can find more about Mintcoin at
http://mintcoin.org

If you want to contribute to Mintcoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/langerhans/mintcoin-wallet-new

This is a fork of the popular Bitcoin Wallet by Andreas Schildbach and was converted to Mintcoin by me.
Find the original at
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.schildbach.wallet
